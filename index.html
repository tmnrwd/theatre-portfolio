About

Tim Norwood is an emerging theatre-maker from Sheffield. He's queer, disabled, and happily mining them both for material. He shares untold and misunderstood stories with audiences to inspire compassion for people that society doesn't really GET, like living on Death Row, or with depression or PTSD.
